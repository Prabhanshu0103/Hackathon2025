Anti-India Campaign Detector

A Digital Disinformation Dashboard

The Problem: A Digital Battleground

Foreign state and non-state actors are running coordinated, large-scale disinformation campaigns to spread false narratives, incite social unrest, and erode public trust. These campaigns have real-world consequences, from social discord to economic instability.

Our Solution: A Proactive Defense System

Our platform is designed to proactively identify, analyze, and counter anti-India disinformation campaigns. It provides a clear, data-driven dashboard to empower fact-checkers, journalists, and government agencies to respond effectively.

  • Keyword-based detection for campaign identification.
  • Real-time analysis to monitor emerging narratives.
  • Sentiment analysis to quantify a campaign's narrative.
  • Propagator identification to pinpoint key accounts and groups.
